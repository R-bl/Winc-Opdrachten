Featured Label

Giegling

Electronic music label based in Weimar, Germany who manufacture & design their own limited covers for each release.

Before it was a label, Giegling was a small club in Weimar, Germany. It had been a student bar for years—the kind of place where you play table tennis and drink watery cocktails—until the owner decided to do something new with it. He approached Konstantin (he preferred that we didn't include his last name), a student at Bauhaus University, and asked if he had any ideas.
